Do you need low cost renderings?

Etivorm is an initiative in constant development, which aims to be the main alternative for all those who require a very low cost rendering, but clear enough for people who see it to understand your work. Therefore, if you require renders that are not hyper-realistic and cost a lot less than twenty dollars each, you are in the right place. 

Process

1 Model preparation

The preparation of the model consists of:

  • You must assign materials to each element of the 3D model that you require to show in the render. 
  • If you want the render to have an environment with buildings, vegetation, people and vehicles you must also implement it in the 3D model. (We suggest that you implement these elements of the environment in the post-production of your render. Because many times the ones that come in the software are of low quality).
  • If you require specific perspectives or have an affinity for certain angles in the perspectives, please take a screenshot of the 3D model in those positions and attach them when compressing the file or setting up the views you require, in your 3D modeling software.
  • In case you agree, continue with phase 2.

Note: Review more details about model preparation and accepted programs.

We support 3D models made in the following software: Sketch up 2021 or lower – Revit 2021 or lower – Archicad 25 or lower – Rhinoceros 7 or lower. If you use other software and want to use our services, you can save it in FBX format. It is up to you what elements you want to be shown in the render; with or without furniture, with nothing, little or a lot of environment. We render your model as you send it, however it is essential that you do the model preparation and compress your file as we indicate, so that your model can be reviewed.

2 Upload file

When you consider that your 3D model is ready to render, compress it and upload your file for us to review it. If everything is in order we will send you a sample render as soon as possible to encourage you to continue with the purchase phase.

Check here the requirements for your file to be reviewed.

Important                  

When uploading the file you will be asked for an email, you must use the same email when making the purchase for verification purposes.

Your 3D model must meet the following requirements in order to be submitted:

  • The name of the 3D model should be as follows: Software name dot a first name a last name dot country; all capitalized. For example: RHINO. ERICK PARKER. UNITED STATES. If you want to put another name and country to protect your privacy do it without problem, however the name of the software must be the authentic one.
  • After you have changed the name of your 3D model compress it with the Freearc program, we leave you a guide on how to download it and configure it to perform an optimal compression. That’s all; you can now upload your file for us to review it.

  • In case you need to send more than one 3d model, you must add the number of renders needed for each model in the name, for example RHINO.ERICK PARKER. UNITED STATES. 5R. Then put the 3D models in the same folder and compress it. 
  • Make sure that the number of renders you are going to buy is equal to the number you indicate in the name of the 3D models you are going to upload.

3 Purchase

If you decide to continue with the purchase phase, you will have to choose the plan that best suits your needs.Once the purchase and verification are completed, you will have to wait the time indicated in the plan before you can access the content.

Check here the requirements for your file to be reviewed.

Remember                                                               

At the time of purchase you have to enter the same email address you used previously to upload your file. If not, you will have to upload it again, with the email you used for the purchase in order to verify it. You can see an example here.

CLASSIC

We have 3 daily plans that allow you to buy in a classic way, this means that you can buy the renders you need at any given time. Our daily plans are classified from lowest to highest cost according to the delivery time.

MEMBERSHIP 

Memberships are monthly plans that reduce the price of the render and have a limit of renders per month. They follow the same process and requirements that have been established for models to be reviewed.
